About Us

As the world moves into a future where technology is integrated into every aspect of our lives;

Our Vision

Is to explore the human element in technology and develop innovative new ways of interfacing and bridging the gap between reality and the virtual world, through creativity and technical excellence.

Our Mission

Is to achieve this end by boldly standing at the crossroads where technology, science, the arts and humanities meet, by combining our skills in game development, web design, general graphics design, mobile app development, audio/video editing, animation and the cutting edge of interactive multimedia. We hope to bring all of this together through placing an added emphasis on creativity, experimentation and innovation.
